APPLICATIONS MODULES

The MX Picker is a high-capacity autonomous mobile robot (AMR) specifically designed for pallet lifting and transportation tasks. With a payload capacity of 1200 kg, the MX Picker is equipped to handle a wide range of pallets and heavy cases with ease. Its multiple lifting functions allow it to adapt to different workflows and environments, making it a flexible solution for automating heavy case and pallet picking workflows. The robot's advanced sensors and navigation systems ensure seamless integration with other machines and equipment in your facility, while its advanced safety features protect your workers during operation. Additionally, the MX Picker is easy to operate and maintain, with an intuitive interface and user-friendly controls. By automating your heavy case and pallet picking workflows with the MX Picker, businesses can achieve increased productivity, improved safety, and better inventory management. The MX Picker is a cost-effective solution that is ideal for businesses of all sizes looking to automate their warehouse operations.
